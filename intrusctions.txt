Intructions to play Blackjack
1) Theres a dealer's hand and a players hand (your cards)
2) You will first get 2 cards
3) Main objective: reach a value of 21 or get the closest to 21 without going over. 
     NOTE: winning/losing is dependent on dealer
4) If dealer's first 2 cards
    - 17 or over: it must stand meaning no more cards for dealer
    - 16 or under: dealer must take a card
5) You don't know dealer's card value until after you decide 
    
    IMPORANT NOTICE: 
        a. Cards with faces = 10 
        b. Ace= 11 or 1 depending on if it will make the value exceed 21
    TERMS
        Bust: went over value of 21 = LOSE
        Hit:requestion additonal card
        Stand: declined additonal cards
